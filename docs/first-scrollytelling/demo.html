3. A simple demo

This section will walk through a simple scrollytelling demo using Maplibre GL JS, a popular open-source mapping library. The demo will include a sticky overlay and scroll-triggered animations to demonstrate the basics of scrollytelling.

A recap of what is happening:

  • The background is the map with full viewport height and position: sticky.
  • The foreground are the text cards.
  • Scrollama is used to trigger events based on scroll position. The offset is 0.5 meaning when the card is halfway through the viewport, it will trigger an event. You can view this change and response in "Scrollama response"
  • The map is being updated based on the card index that is in view

There are lots of different ways to implement scrollytelling and this demo is just one example. Good scrollytelling takes first and foremost a good story and compelling visuals and second, careful attention to the user experience.
